## Abstract

This review provides a comprehensive overview of recent advances in polymer materials science, focusing on structure-property relationships, dynamic bonding mechanisms, and functional applications. We systematically analyze materials systems including polyurethanes, ionogels, hydrogels, piezoelectric polymers, and dynamic covalent networks. Key characterization methods spanning SAXS, DMA, rheology, and computational approaches are evaluated. The review identifies critical scientific questions and proposes future research directions for the field.
