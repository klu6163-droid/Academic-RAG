## 2. Research Background & Significance
Machine learning applications in polymer science are often inefficient due to molecular representations that neglect the inherent
hierarchical and statistical nature of macromolecules. This work introduces a structure-aware graph convolutional network (GCN)

graph representation where nodes correspond to monomer units and explicitly integrates molecular mass distribution (MMD)
