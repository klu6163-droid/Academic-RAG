## 3. Mechanistic Chain

chemical structure (diisocyanate + chain extender + polyol)
    -> thermodynamic incompatibility
    -> microphase separation (hard domains + soft matrix)
    -> hydrogen bonding + lateral stacking in hard domains
    -> under deformation: hard domain reorientation + strain-induced crystallization
    -> strain-induced stiffening at crack tip
    -> enhanced toughness and fatigue resistance
