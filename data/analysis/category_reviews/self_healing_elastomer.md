## 1. Research Background

Self-healing elastomers can recover mechanical damage through dynamic bond exchange (covalent) or physical interaction (hydrogen bonding, ionic, supramolecular). Key challenges include balancing healing efficiency with mechanical strength, and achieving room-temperature healing without external stimuli.

## 3. Core Dynamic Bond / Physical Interaction Mechanisms

**Dynamic Covalent Bonds:**
- Disulfide exchange
- Diels-Alder / retro-Diels-Alder
- Boronate ester exchange
- Imine (Schiff base) exchange
- Transesterification

**Physical Interactions:**
- Hydrogen bonding (UPy, urea, urethane)
- Ionic interactions
- Supramolecular host-guest
- Metal-ligand coordination
