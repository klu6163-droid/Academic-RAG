## 1. Research Background

Polycaprolactone (PCL) is a biodegradable polyester widely used as the soft segment in polyurethane synthesis. PCL-based PUs combine the mechanical properties of polyurethane with the biocompatibility and degradability of PCL. Key parameters include PCL molecular weight, hard/soft segment ratio, and crystallinity, which collectively govern degradation rate, mechanical performance, and biological response.
