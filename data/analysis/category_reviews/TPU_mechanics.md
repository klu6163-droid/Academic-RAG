## 1. Research Background

Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is widely used in applications requiring elasticity, toughness, and processability. Understanding the relationship between microstructure (hard/soft domain morphology, hydrogen bonding network) and mechanical response (tensile, fatigue, fracture) is critical for material design.
